About Mean Normalization

What is Mean Normalization?

Mean normalization is a preprocessing technique that:

  • Calculates the mean of all pixel values in the image
  • Subtracts the mean from each pixel value
  • Centers the data around zero
  • Rescales the result for display (0-255 range)
Why Use Mean Normalization?
  • Removes bias in the data
  • Helps with machine learning model training
  • Reduces the impact of lighting conditions
  • Standardizes input for neural networks
Formula: normalized_pixel = (original_pixel - mean) rescaled to [0, 255]
