About CNN Visualization

AI-Powered Analysis

This tool uses Gemini Vision to analyze your uploaded image, then Gemini 2.0 Flash for text descriptions and DALL-E 3 to generate realistic CNN layer visualizations based on your specific image content.

The 4 CNN Blocks:
Block 1 Edges & Gradients
Detects basic edges, lines, and gradients
Block 2 Patterns & Textures
Combines edges to form patterns and textures
Block 3 Object Parts
Identifies parts of objects (eyes, wheels, etc.)
Block 4 Complete Objects
Recognizes full objects and semantic meaning
How It Works:
  1. Upload your image
  2. AI analyzes the image content
  3. Click blocks to see transformations
  4. Get both text descriptions + AI-generated images
  5. Visualize how CNNs process your image
Educational Tool: This visualization helps understand how deep learning models "see" and process images through hierarchical feature extraction.
Powered by AI: Uses advanced language models to generate detailed descriptions of CNN layer transformations.
